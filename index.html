Homely was created with the goal in mind to ease the homesickness expatriates tend to deal with in their respective foreign countries. We believe that our candles will transport its users back to memories that will remind them of their homecountry, by making use of a person's sense of smell. Our candles are fully customizable to give each customer a personal experience.
